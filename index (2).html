What Abraham Electrician Does

Full-service electrical work, done to code.

From small repairs to whole-home rewiring, here's the work we handle every week.

Wiring & Rewiring

New circuit runs, whole-home rewires, and fixing outdated or unsafe wiring behind the walls.

Panel Upgrades & Breaker Boxes

Replacing old fuse boxes, upgrading amperage, and installing new circuit breakers safely.

Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, chandeliers, outdoor fixtures, and dimmer switches installed and wired properly.

Outlet & Switch Repair

Replacing worn outlets, adding GFCI protection, and fixing switches that spark, buzz, or don't work.

Ceiling Fan Installation

Mounting and wiring ceiling fans, including adding new switches or remote controls.

EV Charger Installation

Dedicated circuits and Level 2 charger installs for home electric vehicle charging.

Generator Installation & Hookup

Backup generator wiring and transfer switch installation so the power stays on when it matters.

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Tracking down flickering lights, tripped breakers, dead outlets, and other electrical gremlins.

Code Compliance & Inspections

Bringing older homes up to current electrical code and prepping panels for inspection or resale.

Surge Protection

Whole-house surge protectors to keep appliances and electronics safe from power spikes.

Smoke & CO Detector Installation

Hardwired smoke and carbon monoxide detectors installed and interconnected for safety.

Residential & Commercial Wiring

New construction, remodels, and commercial tenant build-outs wired from the ground up.
